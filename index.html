About Me

I am a full-stack software developer based in Dubai, UAE, with over 5 years of experience building and maintaining internal web applications and operational systems for large enterprises. For the past 3+ years I have served as lead developer on VAMS, a custom Django-based ERP used across the multi-company Binghalib Group, owning everything from requirements and data modelling to Docker-based deployment and live support.

My core strength is Python back-end development and REST API design, paired with modern TypeScript front-ends in Vue.js and React.js. I work comfortably across the full stack and the full software development lifecycle — designing PostgreSQL and MySQL schemas, implementing SSO and role-based access control, and shipping reliable, well-documented software into production.

Recently I designed and deployed Nexa, an AI automation platform (FastAPI + Claude API) that reads incoming RFQ emails, extracts the key data with AI, and writes it straight into the ERP — cutting hours of manual work from the sales team’s day. I have also built and supported offshore systems for ADNOC, including a remote wellhead monitoring platform deployed on the Zakum and Umm Shaif platforms.

Whether it is a quick landing page, a custom business application, or an end-to-end automation, I focus on understanding the bigger picture and delivering solutions that make a measurable impact. If that sounds like what you need, let’s connect.
